what is this?

Every soundscape here is generated live on your tablet — layers of shaped noise, slow drones, and little scheduled events (a bell, a bird, a rain plink). No recordings, no streaming, no repeats. It works fully offline once pinned to your home screen.

Under each scene hums a quiet binaural undertone — two tones a few hertz apart, one per ear, tuned faster in the morning and slower at night. It only works with headphones; on speakers it politely does nothing. Turn it off below if it's not for you.

Texture adds a faint vinyl crackle and tape wobble, for warmth. Also optional.
